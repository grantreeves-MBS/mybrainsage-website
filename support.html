Support

Get in touch.

My Brain Sage is built and maintained by one person. Email is the way to reach support and every message is read.

Contact

hello@mybrainsage.com

What this channel is for

This is the place for anything to do with the app: a problem you have run into, a question about how something works, a subscription or billing query, a concern about your data or a thought about the app you would like to share.

Because the app is the work of a single developer, a reply may take a little time, but it will come. Clear detail helps: what device you are using, what you were doing when something went wrong and what you expected to happen.

Your data

My Brain Sage stores your information on your own device and nowhere else. Support cannot see your logged data, your scores or your history. If a question depends on what is on your screen, describing it or sending a screenshot is the way to share it.

For the full detail on how data is handled, see the privacy policy.
