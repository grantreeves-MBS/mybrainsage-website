Privacy

Your brain-health data never leaves your phone.

This is the privacy policy for the My Brain Sage app. It is short because the app is built so there is little to say.

My Brain Sage has no server. Your data is not encrypted on our servers. It is not on our servers at all. It stays on your device, under your control.

Last updated 23 May 2026.

What the app stores

Everything you log in My Brain Sage, your daily entries, your scores, your history, your generated brain-health profile, is stored locally on your own device. It is never transmitted to the developer or to any third party. There is no account to create and no cloud database holding your information.

If you choose to use your device's own backup service, such as iCloud or Google Drive, your app data may be included in that backup. That backup is yours, governed by your agreement with Apple or Google, and the developer has no access to it.

What the app does not do

My Brain Sage contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. It does not collect identifiers, location, contacts, or browsing activity. It does not build a profile of you for any purpose other than the brain-health insights shown to you, on your device, inside the app.

Subscriptions and purchases

The app offers a subscription after a free trial. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple or Google through their app stores, and managed using RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. This handles only the information necessary to confirm and maintain your subscription, such as your purchase and renewal status. It is used solely to manage your subscription. It is not used for advertising or tracking, and it does not give the developer access to your brain-health data.

Payment details themselves, your card or account, are handled entirely by Apple or Google. The developer never sees them.

Children

My Brain Sage is intended for adults. It is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age required by their local app store.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page with a new date. The core commitment, that your brain-health data stays on your device, is permanent and will not change.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to the address on the support page.
